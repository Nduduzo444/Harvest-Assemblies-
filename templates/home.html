Our Story

Harvest Assemblies of Christ Global Church was founded in 2008 in the heart of Phase 5, a vibrant community seeking spiritual renewal and purpose. Under the divine calling and leadership of Apostle Malesela, the church began as a humble gathering of believers in a small hall, united by faith, prayer, and a vision for transformation.

From its earliest days, the church has been a beacon of hope — preaching the gospel, nurturing families, and empowering youth through outreach, worship, and discipleship. What started with just a handful of passionate souls has grown into a thriving global ministry, touching lives across South Africa and beyond.

Over the years, Harvest Assemblies has launched dynamic ministries including the Harvest Youth Fire, Women of Grace, and Kingdom Builders Men's Fellowship. Our annual conferences, healing crusades, and community upliftment programs have become pillars of impact and inspiration.

Today, we continue to walk boldly in our calling — raising leaders, restoring families, and reaching nations. Our foundation is Christ, our mission is harvest, and our future is filled with glory.
